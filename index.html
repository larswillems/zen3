    Rendering happens in the background (offscreen) at a steady 60 fps, independent of how fast your tab runs. Each preset defines its own end condition — e.g. "1 HP to Escape" stops a few seconds after the first ball escapes, right when the shatter fades out. The Max value above is just a hard safety cap.
    MP4 uses WebCodecs (H.264 video + AAC audio) via mp4-muxer. Audio is rendered offline from the exact same simulation events you hear live, so every bounce, freeze, and win fanfare lands on the same frame as its visual. PNG frames come with manifest.json and a ready-to-run FFmpeg command (see export_video.py).
